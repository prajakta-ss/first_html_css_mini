Tom And Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a beloved animated series that has entertained audiences for decades with its timeless slapstick humor and classic cat-and-mouse rivalry. Created by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera in 1940, the show follows Tom, a scheming but often bumbling house cat, and Jerry, a clever and mischievous mouse. Their endless chase forms the heart of the series, with elaborate and hilarious scenarios that often result in comedic destruction.
